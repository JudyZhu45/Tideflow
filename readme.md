# TideFlow

## Overview

TideFlow is an innovative task scheduler built on a fundamental premise: **human energy fluctuates like ocean tides throughout the day**. Rather than forcing yourself to follow a rigid schedule, TideFlow helps you align your tasks with your natural energy levels.

- **High-energy tasks** are scheduled during your energy peaks
- **Low-energy tasks** are scheduled during your energy valleys

Instead of relying on complex AI models, TideFlow uses a **mathematically modeled energy curve** and a **rule-based optimization engine** to intelligently arrange your tasks across the day.

## How to Use

### First Time Setup
1. **Complete Onboarding** - Set your energy peak time and customize your preferences
2. **Add Tasks** - Create new tasks or load sample tasks to get started
3. **Optimize Schedule** - Tap "AI Schedule" → "AI Optimize Schedule" to let TideFlow arrange your tasks

### Managing Tasks
- **View Details** - Tap on any task card to see full details
- **Mark Complete** - Double tap a task card to mark it as done
- **Reschedule** - Drag task cards to move them to different time slots
- **Wave Visualization** - View your energy curve and task distribution in real-time

##  Core Concepts

### Energy Curve Model
TideFlow uses a circadian-rhythm-based mathematical model to represent your energy levels throughout the day. The curve adapts to your specified peak hours and smoothly transitions through energy valleys.

### Task Scheduling Algorithm
The rule-based optimization engine considers:
- Task duration and complexity
- Required energy levels
- Your available time slots
- Energy curve patterns

This ensures optimal task placement for maximum productivity and minimal cognitive load.
